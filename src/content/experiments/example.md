---
title: "Constraint: Write Only in the Morning"
date: 2026-05-05
description: "A two-week experiment in time-boxing creative work to the first hour of the day."
---

**Hypothesis:** Writing before checking anything else produces cleaner first drafts.

**Duration:** Two weeks.

**Result:** Mixed. The drafts were rawer, not necessarily better. But the habit felt more honest.

Would try again.
